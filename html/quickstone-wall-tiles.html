Product introduction

What is Quickstone?

MineCore (Former Name: Quartz Core, Silica Mineral Panel) is an eco-friendly building material that can withstand continuous high operation temperatures. It is a light weight, low thermal conductive and high strength product.

Main Components:

Calcium Silicate Board

What is Calcium Silicate Board?

Ingredients are reacted together under heated steam and high-pressure to form the Tobermorite crystals.
The board is cured by an autoclaving process with just water and no added glue and other formaldehyde containing substances.
