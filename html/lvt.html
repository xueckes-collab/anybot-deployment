What is DRY BACK Floor?
Dryback Floor is very soft, it is no click and installed by glue with thickness 2mm-3mm.
 
It is an extremely stable product, in high or low temperature environment, the product is not easy to shrink and it is totally waterproof, therefore very easy to clean. 
 
Dryback flooring is the ideal solution for installing on top of an existing floor. 
Dryback is a very thin but durable floor covering, so it is perfect for anyone covering over an existing floor, or undertaking household renovations.

What is LOOSE LAY Floor?
Loose Lay is a type of luxury vinyl flooring product comprised of not only vinyl, but also phthalate-free plasticizers for added weight and durability---to keep them in place rather than glue or nails. Designed for easy installation, loose lay is generally 5mm in thickness and requires an adhesive only for planks or tiles surrounding the perimeter of the room.
