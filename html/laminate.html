What is Laminate Floor?

Favored by home-owners and design-lovers for their extreme durability and fantastic stain-resistance, laminate floors are synthetic composites made of four layers of materials fused into an incredibly hard surface. Designed to resemble wood, tile and other flooring materials, laminate is much harder and more resistant to wear and tear, fading, staining and moisture. 
 
The four layers of all laminate floors are: Back Layer, Fiberboard Core,Decorative Layer and Wear Layer.
 
One of the best parts of laminate flooring is its ability to appear as attractive and authentic as many different types of real wood flooring without posing some of the wear-and-tear or maintenance issues – and at a fraction of the cost.

FAQ
  • What is Tap&Go locking?

    Tap&Go locking is Yekalon patent locking system, invented in 2006. This is one of the three world patent locking system. It's a symbol of good quality, because only 860-900kg/m3 density HDF can be used to cut the locking. The smart groove design assures the flooring install with only one person and one rubber hummer.

  • What is freshened HDF core?

    Freshened HDF core is made of 15-years of wetland pine, only the bark-offed tree trunk can be used as raw material. This HDF core density can be 880-900kg/m3. No supplier in China can make this good quality.

  • How many levels of products do you have?

    With 17-years of development, Yekalon Bergeim flooring can provide you economical, middle and high-end products to meet all kinds of customers.

  • Do you have other flooring products?

    Except laminate flooring, we have all kinds of laminate flooring mouldings, underlayment. And also we have PVC flooring and accessories, carpets and accessories.

  • Can you give sample supports?
