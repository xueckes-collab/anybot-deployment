What Is QuickStone? QuickStone lightweight wall panels achieve remarkable performance through the combination of a calcium silicate board substrate with pure quartz, wood fiber, lime, Portland cement, and water. Through a unique manufacture, the eco-friendly combination crystallizes massive quantities of Tobermorite, which markedly improves structural strength and stability. It is unique because it neither uses glue nor toxic chemicals in its process. The product is entirely reduced to a hydration polymerization process; hence, it is an entirely formaldehyde-free, low-emission solution, perfect for modern green buildings in health and environmental terms. Three key versions of QuickStone are available, covering a broad spectrum of style and functional needs: QuickStone Digital Printing Wall Panel – Infused with the latest digital textures such as natural stone, rustic wood, and industrial cement, it allows for infinite design freedom. QuickStone EasyTile – These panels are designed for fast and easy installation and can be customized in several tile dimensions and grid layouts. QuickStone Pre-Coated Wall Panel – Designed in sleek solid colours with pre-painting, these are ideal for minimalist- or contemporary-themed spaces. 2. Whether a full-blown renovation or a weekend project, QuickStone offers the flexibility, speed, and accuracy to suit your needs. It is totally clean and dry and thereby avoids mess and increases time to occupancy in commercial settings: a crucial factor, as even a day of downtime means a heavy cost. 7.1 Installation Instructions: Surface is to be washed and leveled, ensuring a solid base. Measure and cut the panels with a diamond saw ensuring absolute precision. Dab the back side of the panel with adhesive in ≥5 cm diameter glue dots for attractive bonding. Install panels from the bottom upwards keeping them aligned using level markings. Finish corners with metal profiles. Fix with screws or adhesive. Use T&G locking for interlocking panels while starting from the male groove side. 7.2 Two installation methods are available: Adhesive mounting is suitable for renovation works allowing an easy direct mounting over existing surface. Light steel keel framing is considered for commercial jobs if the wall structure needs to support heavier loads or in a setting prone to more abuse. 8.
