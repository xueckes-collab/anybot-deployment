Defining Quickstone Wall Tiles Quickstone Wall Tiles is a 100% inorganic, plastic-free building material engineered for interior and exterior wall applications. Unlike traditional drywall, concrete, or plastic-based composites, it is composed of naturally occurring minerals and plant fibers, including: Quartz (for durability and fire resistance) Calcium Silicate (for structural stability) Portland Cement (for water resistance) Plant-Based Fibers (for flexibility and reduced weight) Manufactured through an autoclaving process (high-pressure steam curing), Quickstone tiless are entirely glue-free and formaldehyde-free, aligning with global green building standards. Core Technologies Behind Quickstone Wall Tiles 1. Mineral Core Innovation The heart of Quickstone lies in its patented mineral core, a dense yet lightweight matrix formed by tobermorite crystals. These crystals are created under high-pressure steam, eliminating the need for synthetic adhesives or plastics.
